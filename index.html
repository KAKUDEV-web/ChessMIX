Blunder Prevention

Why we drop pieces and how to stop it.

The "Hope Chess" Syndrome

Why: You make a move hoping your opponent doesn't see the threat, without checking their threats first.

Fix: Before touching a piece, ask: "What is my opponent threatening?" (Blumenfeld's Rule).

Tactical Myopia

Why: Focusing only on your own ideas and ignoring loose pieces.

Fix: Scan the board for undefended pieces every move. If it's loose, it's lost.

Time Trouble Panic

Why: Rushing in the opening to save time for the endgame, leading to unsound positions.

Fix: Develop rhythm. If you don't know the theory, play solid, not fast.
